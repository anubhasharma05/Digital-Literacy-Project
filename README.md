Digital Literacy Project
 About the Project
This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio Project completed as part of the CSE0001 – Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal University.
In this project, I took on the role of a Student Digital Ambassador, where I explored essential digital skills required for academic and professional success. The project is divided into five tasks, each focusing on a different aspect of digital literacy such as online presence, communication, collaboration tools, and cyber safety.
