Social Media Do’s and Don’ts (For College Students)

 Do’s
Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts and personal information.
Think before you post—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
Share useful, positive, and informative content.
Maintain a professional profile, especially on platforms like LinkedIn.
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.

 Don’ts
Do not share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, passwords, OTPs).
Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown messages.
Do not overshare your location or daily activities publicly.
